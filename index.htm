श्रीमंतं जगदुदयस्थितिप्रणाशान् कुर्वंतं गुणगणपूर्णमीशितारं । मुक्तानामपि सकलैरवद्यगंधैरस्पृष्टं हरिमनिशं नमामि भक्त्या ॥

श्री हुलगी श्रीपत्याचार्यप्रणीतः श्री द्वैतद्युमणिः

Dvaita Dyumaṇi is intended to be a Digital Corpus for Dvaita Siddhānta – a repository serving a platform for enthusiasts of Dvaita Siddhānta to access a vast collection of digital resources, including eternal works of Ācāryas, Haridāsas, and other Vidvāns!

Though the work is in progress, in both site building and content publishing, feel free to explore around; and if you’d like to contribute and be part of the project don’t hesitate to connect or hop over to contribution page. [The site looks best in Desktop!]
