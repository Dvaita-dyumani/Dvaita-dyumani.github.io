Dvaita Dyumaṇi is intended to be a Digital Corpus for Dvaita Siddhānta – a repository serving a platform for researchers and enthusiasts of Dvaita Siddhānta to access a vast collection of digital resources, including eternal works of Ācāryas, Haridāsas, and other Vidvāns!

Though the work is in progress, in both site building and content publishing, feel free to explore around. [The site looks best in Desktop!]
